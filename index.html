Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download an Instagram Reel on my phone?
Open Instagram, find the Reel, tap the share icon (➤), and choose Copy Link. Then open this page in your browser, paste the link, and tap Download. On Android you can save directly to storage; on iPhone, save to Files then move to Camera Roll.
Is ReelSave completely free?
Yes. ReelSave is 100% free with no sign-up, no subscription, and no hidden fees. We're supported by non-intrusive ads.
Do downloaded Reels have a watermark?
No. We fetch the original file directly from Instagram's content delivery network. You get the clean file exactly as the creator uploaded it — no watermarks added.
How many Reels can I download per day?
There is no download limit. You can download as many Instagram Reels as you need, completely free and without any caps.
Can I download private Instagram Reels?
No. ReelSave can only access public Reels. Private content requires the account holder's permission and cannot be downloaded by third-party tools.
What video formats are supported?
ReelSave downloads MP4 (video) and MP3 (audio-only extraction). MP4 is compatible with all devices and editing software.
Is it legal to download Instagram Reels?
Downloading for personal offline viewing is generally accepted, but re-sharing, redistributing, or commercialising others' content without permission may violate Instagram's Terms of Service and copyright law. Always respect creators' rights.
